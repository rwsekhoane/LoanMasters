Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible for an online loan?

  • You must be 18 years or older and must have a regular form of income in order to apply for a loan.
  • You must have a South African ID (Identity Document)
  • You must have a bank account

Can I get a loan if I have a bad credit record?

Different loan lenders have different approval ratings. Our lenders will consider clients with all types of credit records and will find a way to approve your loan.

How long does it take to approve my loan application?

This varies by loan provider, in some cases you can get an instant online decision from a lender, while other providers will provide a decision via email, call or sms within a few hours or working days.
